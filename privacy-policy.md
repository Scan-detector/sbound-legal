# Privacy Policy for Sbound

**Effective Date:** 17 January 2026

## Introduction

Sbound ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our mobile application. By using Sbound, you agree to this policy.

## Information We Collect

**Automatically Collected Information:**

- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app, which features you use, and how often
- **Device Information:** Your device type, operating system, and app version
- **Log Data:** Error reports and performance data to help us improve the app
- **Subscription Data:** Your subscription status, trial period, and purchase history (we do not receive your payment details)

**What We Do Not Collect:**

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal contact information. No account is required to use Sbound.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the Sbound app
- Manage your free trial and subscription
- Improve your experience
- Identify and fix technical issues

## Free Trial and Subscription

Sbound offers a 7-day free trial. After the trial ends, a subscription is required to continue using the app.

- Subscriptions are billed monthly or yearly through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store
- You can cancel at any time through your Apple ID or Google Play account settings
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
- We do not process payments directly — all transactions are handled securely by Apple or Google

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is encrypted both in storage and during transmission
- We do not have access to your payment information
- We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law

## Third-Party Services

Sbound uses the following third-party services:

- **Apple App Store / Google Play Store:** Handles purchases, subscriptions, and payment processing. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://apple.com/privacy) and [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **RevenueCat:** Manages subscription and trial status across platforms. See [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://revenuecat.com/privacy)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** a copy of your data
- **Delete** your data
- **Cancel** your subscription through your Apple ID or Google Play account

To make a request, contact us at the email below.

## Children's Privacy

Sbound is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us immediately.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app with an updated effective date.
